My UX Skills

User Experience

Getting to know the users for each individual project is an intensive and fun process. I enjoy conducting interviews and taking a moment to connect with users and identify their needs through active listening and observations.

Wireframing

Taking the needs of the user and designing possible solutions on paper or as digital wireframes is a skill I enjoy. Personally, I like to sketch out ideas on paper first, then use Figma or XD to add more structure to the wireframes as they increase in fidelity.

Prototyping

When it comes to putting the users flow into action, prototyping the wireframes together is really exciting! I enjoy making those connections and seeing how the design comes to life.
